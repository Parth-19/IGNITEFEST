Are you ready for the ultimate test of skill and strategy?! Our Valorant tournament is coming to town. Bring your A-game and battle it out in our Valorant Lan Tournament- Valorant Champions NMIMS. Teams from all around will compete in intense 5v5 single elimination matches where every match counts. With a chance to win cash prizes and bragging rights, this is an event you won't want to miss. So gather your teammates and get ready to dominate the competition!


 

Rules:

● Teams will consist of five players, one of whom will be designated captain and will be the main point

  of contact with the Tournament organizers

● Participants can either register as a team or individually.

● Slots are limited and registrations would be conducted on a first come first serve basis.

● Whole event will be conducted through offline mode. Everyone has to show up in person.

● There will be up to 32 teams (approx) in the tournament

● You can bring your side equipment according to your preference (mouse/keyboard/headphone only).Switching of gear should be done under the guidance of a moderator.

● Every participant must:

  ○ play on their main Riot account

  ○ be able to play on the Mumbai servers of Valorant

● By participating in the Valorant Tournament, participants acknowledge they will, without limitation, comply with the Tournament Rules, Riot's Terms of Service, and with the statements and decisions made by the administration

● Any form of toxicity, vulgarity, racism, sexism, or disrespectful behavior is not appreciated

● Any damage done to the pc’s/equipment of the café by the participants will lead to further consequences.

● Maps and sides will be decided by a map-ban process which will be explained during the Tournament

● Each team will be allowed to take one timeout per match

● Punishments shall be at the administration’s sole discretion

● Unless otherwise stated, the Tournament Round will begin as scheduled. Some rounds may be delayed; for the most up-to-date information, be active on our WhatsApp Servers

● Players must enter the lobby 10 minutes before the game is scheduled to start, failure to do so will lead to disqualification of the respective team and the other team getting a by-win.

● Only the host and other moderators will be allowed to join any match as an observer.

● All decisions made by the administration are final and binding.
