Rules:

Guidelines for the NFT event:

1. Theme: Sustainable energy, blockchain and cryptocurrency, Quantum computing,

Nanotechnology, Fine art, and contemporary art, virtual worlds.

2. Topics/Domains can include(but are not restricted to):

 ● Avatars (Social Media Avatars)

 ● Abstract Art

 ● Collectibles

 ● Franchise Art (Original Series, not a copyrighted one)

 ● Photography

 ● Music

 ● Gamification

 ● One-of-One Artwork

3. Participation: Individually or in a team of 5 members, all participant names should be

registered beforehand, and additions to the team during the event time will not be

allowed.

4. Start date: February 9th, 2023

5. Submission deadline: February 10th, 2023(24hrs)

6. Submission requirements: NFTs must be submitted in the form of .gif, .mp4, .jpg, or

.png files should be no larger than 100MB.

7. Originality (any instance of plagiarism, use of AI generated artwork or the use of Internet

templates will disqualify the contestant), creativity, relevance to the theme, and technical

execution (design and development) are the judging criteria.

8. Submission process: NFTs should be submitted via web form on Ignite's website with a

brief artist's statement.

The participants should provide the following information:

 -Author/authors’ name

 -NFT name

 -Theme chosen

 -artist’s statement(description of NFT)

9. Intellectual property: By submitting an NFT, participants retain the copyright to their

work but grant the organizers the right to use them for promotional or educational

purposes.

10. Any Explicit imagery will be disqualified.
