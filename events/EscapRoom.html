escape  room

Do you love treasure hunts? This event is all about finding clues, solving mysteries, cracking codes with logic and agility. Accomplish your mission with your team and escape!
 

Rules:

  • Entry fee Rs.40 per team.
  • Team should consist of 4 members.
  • Each team will get 20 minutes inside the room.
  • No cellphones allowed.
  • Team should proceed to each stage with all the members.
  • the decorum of the room should be maintained.
  • Unnecessary hampering with the room decoration will lead to disqualification.
  • You will get 3 hints, which when taken will reduce time as: - for 1st hint - 1min, 2nd hint - 3 mins and 3rd hint - 5 mins.
  • Players are expected not to disclose clues or puzzles after playing.
  • Online and offline registrations are available.
  • Final decision will be of the organizers in case of any dispute.
